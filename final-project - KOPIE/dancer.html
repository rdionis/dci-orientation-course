Dancing makes me happy. It always has, since I was a little girl who wanted to be a ballerina. I did dance ballet for over 20 years but was never a professional ballerina. After ballet came contemporary dance, then jazz, then belly dance... And finally, salsa. Salsa took up a major role in y life and at some point I was part of three dance groups at the same time: EDSAE, iDance, Salsa Stage. Back then, I created my own dance fusion solo piece called "Início".
